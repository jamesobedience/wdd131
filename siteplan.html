Site Name

Nigerian Cuisine was chosen to highlight Nigeria's rich and diverse food culture. The name is simple, memorable, and directly reflects the site's purpose: exploring and celebrating the flavors of Nigeria.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to educate and inspire visitors by sharing information about traditional and modern Nigerian dishes, ingredients, regional cooking styles, and the cultural significance of food in Nigeria. The site will include recipe descriptions, photos, and regional highlights to engage both local and international audiences.

Scenarios

  • What are the most popular Nigerian dishes I can try at home?
  • How does cuisine differ between Northern and Southern Nigeria?
  • Where can I find authentic Nigerian ingredients near me?

Color Schema

#006400 - Deep Green (used for headers and accents)
#FFD700 - Warm Gold (used for highlights and background tones)

The deep green represents growth and the lushness of Nigeria's agriculture, while the warm gold reflects energy, richness, and tradition.

Typography

Primary Font: Poppins - used for headings and navigation.

Secondary Font: Open Sans - used for paragraphs and general body content.
